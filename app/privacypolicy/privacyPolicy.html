BuffLog Privacy Policy

This app respects your privacy. We only collect and store the minimum data necessary to provide core functionality.

When you use the app without logging in, all your data is stored locally on your device.

If you choose to log in, your data may be stored securely in the cloud using Firebase services to enable backup and restore functionality.

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

Authentication (such as email and password) is handled securely by Firebase. We do not have access to your password.

You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

This app may use third-party services (such as Firebase) which have their own privacy policies.

By using this app, you agree to this privacy policy.
