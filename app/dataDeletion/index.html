BuffLog Account & Data Deletion

Users of BuffLog may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

To request deletion, please send an email to:

dietcodedev@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your BuffLog account.

Upon verification, your account and associated cloud-stored data will be permanently deleted.

Some limited data may be temporarily retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or operational purposes for up to 30 days.

Locally stored data on your device can also be removed by uninstalling the app.
